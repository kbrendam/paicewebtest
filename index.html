About PAICE

The decision-makers actually deploying AI. Not debating it.

The Pharma AI Commercial Exchange (PAICE) brings together 400+ senior Commercial, Marketing, and IT/Data leaders from pharma and life sciences for two high-intensity days in New Jersey. The agenda is unsparing: how do you connect AI investment to commercial ROI? How do you break the business-IT deadlock? How do you build the data infrastructure that makes omnichannel HCP engagement precise, scalable, and accountable at enterprise scale?

This is not a conference about AI's potential. PAICE is where the people responsible for making it real come to solve the problems no vendor brochure addresses. They leave with the peer benchmarks, commercial frameworks, and direct connections that actually move the needle.
